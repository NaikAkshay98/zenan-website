About Zenan Glass

We've been helping businesses make a lasting impression through custom glass products and branded merchandise for over a decade.

Our Story
Built on Quality
and Trust

Zenan Glass was founded with a simple idea: that every brand deserves premium products that truly represent who they are. What started as a small custom glassware operation has grown into a full-service branded merchandise company serving clients across Canada and the United States.

Over the years we've developed deep expertise in custom etching, printing, embroidery, and sourcing. Our team understands that when you put your logo on something, it reflects your company's standards — so we hold ours to the highest level.

From boutique wineries to Fortune 500 companies, we've helped hundreds of businesses create branded products they're proud of. We're small enough to give every client personal attention, and experienced enough to handle any scale of order.

What We Do
Our Specialties

UV Printing

Ultra-vivid full-colour UV printing directly onto any surface. Sharp detail, fade-resistant, and works on virtually any material.

Screen Printing

Classic, durable screen printing for high-volume orders. Bold ink coverage on apparel, bags, and promotional products.

Decal Printing

Custom cut decals and stickers for branding, packaging, and product labelling. Waterproof and built to last.

Nucleation

Laser-etched nucleation points in glassware for superior carbonation and a perfect pour — ideal for beer and sparkling beverage brands.

Embroidery

Premium thread embroidery for uniforms, hats, bags, and apparel. Consistent stitch quality that holds up wash after wash.

Custom Boxes

Branded packaging and custom boxes designed to impress. Perfect for gifting, kitting, retail, and product launches.

What We Stand For
Our Core Values

Precision

Every detail matters. We verify proofs, check production quality, and don't ship anything we wouldn't be proud to put our own name on.

Partnership

We treat every client like a long-term partner, not a transaction. We're invested in your success and work to earn your repeat business.

Transparency

Clear pricing, honest timelines, and no surprises. You'll always know where your order stands and exactly what you're getting.
